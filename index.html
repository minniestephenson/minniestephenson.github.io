Hi! I'm Minnie Stephenson

Minnie Stephenson is a current sophomore at the Philip Merrill College of Journalism at the University of Maryland. She is on a path of multimedia journalism. Minnie is originally from Marshfield, Massachusetts.

Outside of class, she has worked at multiple organizations. She has been a copy editor and photographer for Stories Beneath the Shell, an on-campus publication, and is currently a staff writer there. She is a current copy editor and former freelance reporter at The Diamondback, another notable on-campus publication. She has also done freelance reporting for WMUC, the on-campus radio station.

Minnie is a part of the Media, Self, and Society College Park Scholars program at the university and is currently working on a media literacy outreach team to teach local middle schoolers about journalism and proper use of social media.

Minnie is passionate about photojournalism and runs her own photography Instagram account, @minniestephensonphoto. She has experience with numerous social media platforms, as well as Adobe Lightroom and Photoshop.
